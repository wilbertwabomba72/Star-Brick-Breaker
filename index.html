Gameplay Highlights

Ball Brick Quest combines classic ball-and-paddle gameplay with modern visual effects and objective-based level design. Collect all stars, preserve your three lives, and smash through layered brick formations while taking advantage of explosive chain reactions.

  • Collect stars to clear each stage completely.
  • Use bomb bricks to wipe out nearby targets.
  • Crack ice bricks in multiple hits.
  • React to moving bricks that change the angle of every shot.
  • Track progress with local save data and level star ratings.

Description

Enter a glowing underwater arcade where every shot matters.

Ball Brick Quest is a single-player brick breaking game built around collectible stars, explosive bomb bricks, moving targets, and icy obstacles. Clear all stars, survive with your remaining lives, and push through 50 progressively harder levels.

Features:
- 50 offline levels with increasing challenge
- Collectible stars in every stage
- Bomb bricks with area explosions
- Ice bricks that require extra hits
- Moving bricks that shift your shot timing
- Local progress saving and level star ratings
- Background music and sound controls
- Optimized for both iPhone and iPad

Perfect for quick sessions, score chasing, and classic arcade fans who want a fresh visual theme.
