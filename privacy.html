1. Information We Collect

Ball Brick Quest does not require account registration and does not ask you to submit personal profile information directly inside the game. The app stores gameplay progress locally on your device, such as unlocked levels, earned stars, and audio settings.

On supported devices, the app may display a tracking permission request before using certain advertising-related identifiers.

3. Local Storage

The game stores certain information locally on your device so that your progress is preserved between sessions. This may include:

  • Unlocked levels

4. How Information Is Used

Information processed by the app or its integrated SDKs may be used to:

  • Save and restore gameplay progress
  • Load and display advertisements
  • Protect the app environment and improve stability
  • Measure app and ad performance

5. Third-Party Services

Ball Brick Quest may integrate third-party advertising, analytics, or security tools. Those providers may handle information according to their own privacy policies and applicable law.

6. Children

The app is intended for a general audience. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that personal information has been provided improperly, please contact the developer.

7. Your Choices

You may be able to limit certain data use by:

  • Adjusting tracking permissions in your device settings
  • Managing ad-related privacy options available on your device
  • Deleting the app to remove locally stored game data from the device
